What's the redesign scope?

How far do we take the sutera.ch inspiration for the new allone.ge landing page?

A

Full Rebrand — Adopt Sutera's Aesthetic

Monochrome palette, viewport-relative typography, scattered organic layouts, loading screen, minimal header. Drop the 3D robot, electric blue, tubelight nav. allone.ge becomes a Swiss-minimal design studio site.

Risk: Loses allone's current identity. Sutera is a personal portfolio — allone is an agency selling services.

B

Selective Fusion — Best of Both

Take Sutera's strongest moves — fluid viewport typography, tight tracking, generous whitespace, organic project layout, Lenis smooth scroll — and blend with allone's identity (accent blue, agency positioning, modern tech feel). New landing page structure but still recognizable as allone.

Sweet spot: Elevates sophistication while keeping brand identity.

C

Mood Only — Keep Current Architecture

Keep current structure/sections but apply Sutera's spacing, typography tightness, and animation philosophy. Same components, refined execution. More of a polish pass than a redesign.

Safest: Minimal work, incremental improvement.
